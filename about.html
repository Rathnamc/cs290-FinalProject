About

I have just completed a Bachelors degree in Computer Science from Oregon State University. I enjoy all areas of the technology Industry, but I am particularly interested in iOS development particuarly using Swift. I have experience with a many languages and development tools including Swift, C/C++, Objective-C, HTML, CSS, XCode, Google App Engine, Code::Blocks, and GitHub. The database stores the users information as well as the top 250 movies from IMDB.

I conducted semi-structured interviews, gathered data, produced low and high fidelity prototypes, and shared our code to create a complete product.

Currently, I work as an IT Engineer in a medical office where my responsibilities range from training employees, keeping EHR services up to date, as well as troubleshooting any equipment. At this point in my career, I am ready to expand my knowledge beyond IT and branch into software development. I am very excited for my first job as a software engineer and to become an asset to the technology community.
